What We Do

Electrical Services You Can Count On

From new construction wiring to panel upgrades and everything in between — Harrington Electric handles it all for homes and businesses across western Oklahoma.

⚡

Residential Wiring

New construction wiring, rewiring older homes, and everything in between. We make sure your home's electrical system is safe, code-compliant, and built to last.

🔌

Panel Upgrades

Outdated breaker panel causing tripped circuits or safety concerns? We upgrade panels to handle modern electrical loads — fast, clean, and done right.

Commercial Electrical

Office buildings, retail spaces, warehouses — we handle commercial electrical work across the western OKC metro with the same care we bring to every home.

Safety & Code Work
