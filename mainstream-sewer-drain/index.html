Our Services

From clogged drains to full sewer line replacements, MainStream handles residential and commercial plumbing jobs across the entire OKC metro. Licensed, bonded, and insured -- with 35+ years of hands-on experience.

🚿

Sewer & Drain Cleaning

Complete drain clearing for all pipe sizes, from 1.5" bathroom drains to 6" main sewer lines. We clear stubborn clogs fast so your water flows again.

📹

Video Inspection & Locating

See exactly what's going on inside your pipes. Our sewer camera inspections pinpoint cracks, blockages, and root intrusions -- no guesswork, no unnecessary digging.

🔧

Sewer Line Repair & Replacement

Broken or collapsed sewer line? We handle full excavation and replacement with minimal disruption to your property. Permanent fixes, not band-aids.

💧

Slab Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden leaks under your foundation can cause serious damage. We locate and repair slab leaks quickly using proven detection methods -- saving your home and your water bill.

🔥

Water Heater Service

Cold showers aren't an option. We repair and replace water heaters -- gas or electric -- with same-day service available so your hot water is back fast.

🛡

Gas Leak Detection & Repair

Smell gas? Don't wait. Our technicians respond quickly to locate and fix gas leaks safely, protecting your family and your property.
