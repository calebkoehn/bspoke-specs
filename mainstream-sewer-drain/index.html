About Us

Why Choose MainStream Sewer and Drain Service

MainStream Sewer and Drain Service, a division of Stone Creek Plumbing Co Inc, has been serving the Oklahoma City metro area for over 35 years. Founded by Greg Mullin in 1989, we've built our reputation on honest work, fair pricing, and treating every home like our own.

Unlike the big franchises, we don't charge trip fees, service fees, or hidden "by the foot" charges. You get a free estimate, upfront pricing, and a team that actually answers the phone. We welcome second opinions — because we're confident in our work.
